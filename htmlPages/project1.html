Easy Learning Platform Using React JS

NishEd – Where Learning Adapts to You.

NishEd is a personalized educational platform designed to simplify and enrich your learning journey.

Built using ReactJS, NishEd is not just a website—it's your gateway to an intuitive and custom-tailored learning experience.

Unlike traditional course platforms, NishEd puts you in control:

  • Courses are taught your way — structured by you, tailored to your style.
  • Video-based and live classes enhance interactivity and retention.
  • A clean, modular UI makes learning seamless and distraction-free.
  • Google Classroom integration (coming soon) for real-time coordination.
  • Easy navigation and guided paths make complex topics approachable—even fun.

Whether you're a student or a mentor, NishEd makes education more accessible, customized, and modern.

About the Project

NishEd is a next-gen educational platform designed to revolutionize the way people learn online. By focusing on personalization and simplicity, NishEd empowers users to choose how, what, and when they learn. The platform is ideal for self-learners, mentors, and anyone looking for a flexible learning journey.

With modern technologies like React JS and upcoming integrations such as Google Classroom, the project aims to bridge the gap between traditional learning systems and digital-first, learner-focused solutions.
